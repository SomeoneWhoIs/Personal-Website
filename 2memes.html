Troll Face

Ah, so let's have a look at the first meme on this list:

This meme was first created in September 2008.

The creator was Carlos Ramirez, who was 18-years old.

The troll face was originally drawn in Microsoft Paint.

It started to gain popularity after Ramirez posted it on 4chan, and eventually it spread to Reddit in 2009, and later made its way to other sites like Facebook. Today, it is basically the universal symbol for internet trolling.

Rickrolling

  • Rickrolling is a type of trolling where a disguised hyperlink will send people to the Never Gonna Give you Up Music video
  • It first started on 4chan, where people would "duckroll"
  • That is, they would send a hyperlink that seemed to be unrelated but it would bring them to a picture of a "duckroll" or a duck on wheels.
    • A duckroll.
  • Then, one day someone on 4chan "duckrolled" someone but instead of linking the classic duck on wheels, they linked Never Gonna Give You Up, and Rickrolling was born
  • Rickrolling reached a peak around 2008, but is still going strong today
